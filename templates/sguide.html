Guidelines



  • Students can see the Live Room Allotment process with the current room status on their respective Dashboards
  • Before registeration every student has to book for particular time slot along with mess-type details to the warden through the Bookings module of their portal
  • Students having queries can chat with the warden by joining either of Allotment-Room_Query-Mess-Roomate room in accordance of their query type using the Query module of the portal
  • Students can register for rooms available on their Dashboard in accordance to the Live Room Status
  • In the Live Room Status only the rooms which are green are available for allotment otherwise the red rooms are laready booked room and are not available for booking
